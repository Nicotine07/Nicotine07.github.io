Reciepe #1

  • British Breakfast
    • Time: 10 minutes
    • Serves: 1
    Ingredients Quantity
    Slice of Breads 4
    Eggs 2
    Sausage 2
    Navel 2
    Glass of Milk 1
    Tomato 1/2
    Beans a few

    Step 1: Toast the slices of bread for a couple of minutes. Four slices will be enough.

    Step 2: While tosting breads, heat the pan and throw a bit of butter into the pan.

    Step 3: When the pan gets ready, grabs 2 eggs and fry them. Make sure you season the eggs with salt and pepper while frying. The eggs are ready when they are half fried.

    Step 4: While the eggs are in the pan, slice the sausages into pieces. This will help you to finish faster.

    Step 5: Then, fry the pieces of sausages you sliced.

    Step 6: These guys are cut in half along the equator and then seared in the pan and seasoned with salt and pepper. They aren’t really cooked, just given a little bit of color.

    Step 7: Open the can of beans and warm in a small pot over low heat, stirring occasionally.

    Step 8: Scoop the beans in the middle of the plate then add the naval at 1-2 o’clock, add the sausages at 3 o’clock, then the eggs at 6. Then fill the rest of the plate with the tomatoes at 11 o’clock. Toasted bread can get tucked in wherever or placed on a side plate.

    Step 9: Don’t forget a glass of milk for drink.

    Now, you have prepared a perfect British Breakfast. Enjoy!

    Source: Full English Website

    Recipe #2

  • Egg Fried Rice
    • Time: 15 minutes
    • Serves: 1
    Ingredients Quantity
    Left over Rice a bowl
    Eggs 2
    Scallion 2
    Oil & Soy Source a few

    Step 1: Before you begin, preheat the wok.

    Step 2: Add oil, remove from heat, and add the beaten egg.

    Step 3: Add more oil and the rice.

    Step 4: Flip the egg and start stir-frying.

    Step 5: Season the rice.

    Step 6: Add the scallions.

    Recipe #3

  • Pancakes
    • Time: 25 minutes
    • Serves: 1
    Ingredients Quantity
    Blueberries 2 and half cups
    Eggs 1
    Milk 1 cup
    Flour 1 1/2 cup
    Sugar & Baking Powder 2tbsp
    Kosher salt half tbsp

    Step 1: In a blender, blend all ingredients through salt, except for additional fruit or corn for cooking, until smooth.

    Step 2: Transfer batter to a bowl and stir in whole fruit or corn kernels.

    Step 3: Heat a 12-inch nonstick skillet on medium until hot; brush lightly with melted butter. In batches, scoop batter by 1/4-cupfuls into skillet. Cook 2 to 3 minutes or until bubbles appear and edges are dry. With a spatula, turn; cook 2 minutes more.
